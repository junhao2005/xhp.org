Waste collection

Our organization is in charge of organizing different outings, throughout the year, on beaches, forests or in the city itself. In these, the main objective is to collect all kinds of waste in garbage bags. Our aim is to keep the environment as clean as possible and that people can enjoy natural landscapes without being polluted by humans. This work is carried out by volunteers registered on our website.

Tree planting

We also take care to plant trees with the permission of the municipalities. Humans need oxygen to be able to live and plants are the ones that provide us with it. Therefore, due to the great deforestation produced during the last decades, we are forced to plant as many trees as possible. With this work we seek to increase the green areas in the peninsula and the conservation and preservation of natural resources.

Environmental education

Finally, our NGO is in charge of carrying out different workshops and talks in educational centers. We believe that education is the fundamental basis for a change towards a better future. Therefore, we carry out exhibitions and activities on climate change, the human being as a cause and the need for change. We mainly focus on primary and secondary schools as we believe that young people need to become aware of the serious situation and let their families know about it.
