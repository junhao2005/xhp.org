Animal rescue service

Due to the increase of abandoned animals in our country, our organization has a rescue service for these animals. Anyone who finds any species of animal abandoned or out of its natural habitat can call the phone number listed on our website. Our organization will be in charge of sending, as soon as possible, a service of experts to rescue the animal with the maximum speed and security possible.

Veterinary care

Once the endangered animals are rescued by our service, explained in the previous section, we provide veterinary care. We have the help of a team of veterinarians, specialized in animals at risk. They have special spaces with all the necessary instruments to guarantee the health of the animals. Above all, this service will be necessary for those animals that have been abandoned for a long period of time or that have suffered accidents and serious injuries.

Animal protection organizations

When the health of the animals has improved or is in good condition, we have different shelters throughout the peninsula. These establishments will serve as a home for them, since they have everything they need. There the animals will have large fields, places to sleep, eat and be comfortable. The people in charge of the place will be in charge of finding a new family for each animal as soon as possible.
