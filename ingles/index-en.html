Who are we?

We are an ONG called XHP. We are dedicated mainly to animal welfare and the care and protection of the environment, as these are two indispensable aspects of our planet. Because of our concern for the fauna and flora, we are very motivated to undertake this project to contribute our grain of sand.

Objective of our ONG

The purpose of our NGO, "XHP", is to promote the protection and conservation of wildlife and natural ecosystems. Our organization works tirelessly to rescue and rehabilitate animals at risk, combat abuse and neglect, and promote sustainable practices that minimize human impact on the environment through awareness campaigns, environmental education and ecological restoration projects, we seek to generate a positive change in society, encouraging people to adopt more responsible and respectful behaviors with nature. In addition, we collaborate with governments and communities to implement conservation policies and protect critical habitats, ensuring a viable future for both animals and humans who depend on a healthy planet.
